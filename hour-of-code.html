Homework 2: Hour of Code

Event Overview

The event took place at School No.7, named after M. Rahim, in Baku, on November 5, from 16:30 to 17:30. There were 21 participants , all 5th-grade students. It was their first time experiencing such an event, and they were very excited.

What happened during the event?

Before this event, our team decided on roles and prepared a presentation. We faced some technical problems during the event. There was an poor internet connectivity. However, after we solved this issue, we faced with another problem,the computer, which was connected to the projector, still coulnd't connect to the internet. Finally, we decided to show everything on our own computers.

The event included:

  • Introducing ourselves and ADA University
  • Discussiing programming basics
  • Guiding students through solving simple programming puzzles

At the end of the event, students received certificates for their participation and efforts.

Acknowledgments

We all want to thank all the staff of School No. 7, named after M. Rahim. They provided great support and helped make the event successful.
